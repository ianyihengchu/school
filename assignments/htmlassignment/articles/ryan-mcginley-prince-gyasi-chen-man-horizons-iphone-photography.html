Ryan McGinley, Prince Gyasi and Chen Man on chasing light with an iPhone

By Thom Waite

The photographers discuss their new exhibition, Horizons, which celebrates 15 years of iPhone photography

Ryan McGinley, Prince Gyasi, and Chen Man are renowned photographers featured in the Horizons exhibition at Apple's new store in London. The exhibition showcases 15 years of iPhone photography, highlighting how mobile technology has transformed the art form. Each photographer brings their unique perspective: - Ryan McGinley is known for his youthful, naturalistic style - Prince Gyasi creates vibrant, stylized portraits celebrating Ghanaian culture - Chen Man pushes boundaries with surreal, futuristic imagery The photographers discuss how iPhone cameras have impacted their work, allowing for spontaneity and new creative possibilities. They also share insights on chasing perfect light and finding inspiration. Horizons aims to inspire visitors to explore iPhone photography themselves. It demonstrates how accessible technology has democratized photography as an artistic medium.
